Mapping Knowledge Flows Between Nonprofit Organizations

This is an interactive visualization of all entities that the Port Phillip EcoCentre in Australia has developed programs or partnerships with.

The radius of each circle represents the number of partnership hours with the respective organization The direction of the arrows show the direction of the partnership.

Hover over nodes to learn more about them, and feel free to pan and zoom!
